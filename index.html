How Can I Help?

Hello, I'm Steven Howard, a Portsmouth, NH based web developer. I've worked in many different roles helping to solve business problems at fortune 100 companies as well as at start-ups. I would like the opportunity to talk to you about your needs, and how I can help your company.

Web Development

Accelerate your business. Whether it's a WordPress site leveraging plug-ins your company has decided to use for eCommerce, or a site to enable integration among the various parts of your business, I want to find out what you need, and how that goal can be realized.

Data Integration

Data is the lifeblood of business. Whether it's data associated with customers, orders, products, inventory, or whatever it is, being able to enter,to view, to output, and to trust the data and the reporting is key to a good night's sleep.

Agile

I'll look through your eyes, what is it that you want built? If you look at the deliverable and want to change it, that's part of the process, our understanding of what's possible evolves, and the engagement can take that into account.

Let me provide value

Take the next step, contact me. I want to understand what challenges you're facing and see how we can move your business forward.
